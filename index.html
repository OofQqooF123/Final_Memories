Final Memories

By: Anotine Miko Dellosa

 

As the sun goes down,

I can see my soul hiding in the shadows.

No resasons were given

That's how life goes.

 

Perhaps this is how it ends.

As I try to fend

Against the shadoes in the darkness,

I try to end my feelings of dread.

 

While the darkness grows,

A doorway opens.

Here is not forever

And life always ends.
